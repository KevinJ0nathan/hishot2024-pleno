ABOUT HISHOT

HISHOT, which stands for HIMTI Seminar-Workshop and Study Tour, is an annual event organized by HIMTI Bina Nusantara. This year, HISHOT 2024 aims to enrich participants' knowledge and experience, focusing on the theme of "From Cyber Security to Cyber Resilience".By joining and participating in HISHOT 2024, attendees gains the exclusive access to engage with experts, practitioners, and peers to gain valuable insights and develop essential skills to navigate the digital world securely and prepare for a successful career in cyber security.

HISHOT, which stands for HIMTI Seminar-Workshop and Study Tour, is an annual event organized by HIMTI Bina Nusantara. This year, HISHOT 2024 aims to enrich participants' knowledge and experience, focusing on the theme of "From Cyber Security to Cyber Resilience".By joining and participating in HISHOT 2024, attendees gains the exclusive access to engage with experts, practitioners, and peers to gain valuable insights and develop essential skills to navigate the digital world securely and prepare for a successful career in cyber security.

HISHOT 2024 encompasses a variety of events aimed at enhancing cyber resilience. The lineup includes:

  1. 1. An onsite seminar focused on the new AI-integrated era in cyber security.
  2. 2. Two online workshops, one introducing participants to the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification, and another addressing protection against cyber threats and phishing for web security.
  3. 3. Study tours, with one local visit to Google Jakarta and an international trip to Malaysia.

The series of event will be held from June-Desember, with the exact date as follow:


  1. Seminar (onsite):
  2. 1. Introducing Secure Cyber Futures: Examining The New AI-Integrated Era - June 22nd 2024

  3. Workshop (online):
  4. 1. Introduction to Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) - July 27th 2024
  5. 2. Protecting Cyber Threats and Phishing for Web Security- August 3rd 2024

  6. Study Tour:
  7. 1.STDN - Google Jakarta. Saturday, 21 September 2024
  8. 2. STLN - Malaysia. Thursday, 5 December 2024 - Sunday, 8 December 2024
